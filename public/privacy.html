1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Pixshop ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, processes, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Images and Media Content

When you use our AI-powered editing features, we temporarily process:

  • Photos and images you upload for editing
  • Generated and modified images created through our AI services
  • Image metadata (excluding location data unless explicitly provided)

Important: Images are processed temporarily and are not permanently stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them to our cloud services (if available).

2.2 Usage Information

  • App usage analytics and performance metrics
  • Feature usage patterns and preferences
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen resolution)
  • Face Data and Photo Processing Our app may process photos containing faces for the purpose of providing editing features, such as beautification and filter effects. All processing occurs locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share biometric identifiers (such as facial recognition templates or geometry). Processed data is used only to provide the requested feature and is discarded immediately after processing. We do not share face data with any third parties.

2.3 Account Information

  • App Store account information (managed by Apple)
  • Subscription status and purchase history (through App Store)
  • User preferences and settings

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address (for API requests only)
  • App version and build information
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Network connection type

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Process images through AI editing and generation services
  • Provide personalized editing suggestions and features
  • Maintain and improve App functionality
  • Enable premium features for subscribers

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Optimize AI model performance and accuracy
  • Develop new features and improvements
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

3.3 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respond to legal requests and court orders
  • Protect our rights and prevent misuse
  • Ensure content policy compliance

4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

4.1 AI Processing Services

We use the following third-party AI services to provide image editing capabilities:

Replicate (Primary AI Provider)

  • Purpose: AI image generation and editing
  • Data Shared: Images uploaded for processing
  • Data Retention: Images are processed temporarily and deleted after processing
  • Privacy Policy: https://replicate.com/privacy

OpenRouter (Fallback AI Provider)

  • Purpose: Alternative AI image generation when primary service is unavailable
  • Data Shared: Images uploaded for processing
  • Data Retention: Images are processed temporarily and not stored
  • Privacy Policy: https://openrouter.ai/privacy

4.2 App Store Integration

Subscription and purchase data is managed by Apple through the App Store. We do not directly handle payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all App Store transactions.

4.3 Analytics Services

We may use anonymous analytics to understand app usage patterns. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure API connections to third-party services
  • Limited data retention periods
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

5.2 Image Processing Security

  • Images are processed temporarily and deleted after completion
  • No permanent storage of user images on our servers
  • Secure transmission to AI processing services
  • Content filtering to prevent inappropriate material

6. Data Retention

6.1 Image Data

Images uploaded for editing are:

  • Processed temporarily during editing sessions
  • Automatically deleted after processing completion
  • Not stored permanently unless you explicitly save to device
  • Removed from third-party AI services after processing

6.2 Usage Data

  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 2 years
  • Error logs: Retained for up to 1 year
  • Account preferences: Retained while account is active

You have the right to:

7.2 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Note: We do not sell personal information to third parties.

7.3 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
